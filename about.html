Who We Are

Technology-enabled insurance facilitation

Paisabada™ is a technology-enabled insurance facilitation platform designed to simplify how individuals and families explore and access insurance products. We combine structured digital systems with guided human assistance to support informed understanding of insurance options.

Our Approach

A disciplined and compliance-conscious approach

Our approach focuses on transparency, structured processes, and responsible facilitation where technology reduces friction and user trust is built through consistency.

How We Work

Structured and accountable support

  • Structured onboarding with secure verification
  • Assistance in accessing insurance products offered by IRDAI-licensed insurers
  • Digital policy visibility and reminders
  • Post-purchase support through structured processes
Operating Structure

Two-entity operating model

Paisabada operates under a structured two-entity model. The technology platform is operated by Wealtrix Fintech Pvt. Ltd., responsible for digital infrastructure and systems.

Insurance facilitation services are provided by Paisabada Financial Services (Proprietorship Firm), acting as an authorised POS insurance facilitator for IRDAI-licensed insurers.

Paisabada does not provide financial or investment advice and operates strictly within the scope of its authorised facilitation framework.

Paisabada does not underwrite insurance policies. All products are issued and administered by respective insurance companies.

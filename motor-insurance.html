Motor Insurance

Vehicle protection plans facilitated through authorised insurers

1. Overview

Motor insurance provides financial protection against damage, theft or third-party liability involving your vehicle.

Policies are issued by IRDAI-licensed insurance companies and facilitated through Paisabada Financial Services.

2. Types of Motor Insurance

  • Third Party Liability Insurance
  • Comprehensive Motor Insurance
  • Standalone Own Damage Cover

Coverage details and benefits vary depending on insurer and policy selected.

3. Key Coverage Areas

  • Accidental vehicle damage
  • Theft protection
  • Third-party liability coverage
  • Natural disaster protection (depending on policy)

4. Policy Issuance

Motor insurance policies are issued by respective insurers after proposal review and underwriting.

Paisabada Financial Services facilitates comparison, documentation assistance and purchase through authorised insurer channels.

5. Claims Process

Customers may raise claims directly with the issuing insurer.

Paisabada may assist in documentation and claim coordination where required.

Final claim approval and settlement decisions are taken solely by the insurance company.

6. Important Disclosure

Insurance is the subject matter of solicitation. Please read policy documents carefully before purchase.

Premium, coverage, exclusions and claim settlement are governed by insurer policy terms.

Paisabada Financial Services is an Authorised POS Insurance Facilitator. Insurance policies are issued by respective IRDAI-licensed insurance companies. We do not underwrite insurance policies or guarantee claim settlement. Final underwriting and claim decisions rest solely with the respective insurer.

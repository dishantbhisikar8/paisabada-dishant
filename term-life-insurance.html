Term Life Insurance

Financial protection plans for your family

1. Overview

Term life insurance provides financial protection to the nominee in case of the policyholder’s death during the policy term.

Policies are issued by IRDAI-licensed insurance companies.

2. Key Features

  • High sum assured coverage
  • Affordable premiums
  • Flexible policy terms
  • Optional riders depending on insurer

3. Eligibility

Eligibility criteria such as age, income and medical requirements vary depending on the insurer and policy selected.

4. Policy Issuance

Term life insurance proposals are reviewed by insurers based on underwriting guidelines.

Paisabada Financial Services facilitates comparison and policy purchase through authorised insurer partners.

5. Claim Settlement

Nominees may submit claims directly to the issuing insurer.

Paisabada may assist with documentation and claim coordination where required.

Final claim settlement is determined by the insurer.

6. Important Disclosure

Insurance is the subject matter of solicitation. Please read policy documents carefully before purchase.

Paisabada Financial Services is an Authorised POS Insurance Facilitator. Insurance policies are issued by respective IRDAI-licensed insurance companies. We do not underwrite insurance policies or guarantee claim settlement. Final underwriting and claim decisions rest solely with the respective insurer.

Personal Accident Insurance

Protection against accidental risks

1. Overview

Personal accident insurance provides financial protection in case of accidental death or disability.

Policies are issued by IRDAI-licensed insurance companies.

2. Coverage Scope

  • Accidental death benefit
  • Permanent total disability
  • Permanent partial disability
  • Medical expenses (policy dependent)

3. Benefits

Benefits depend on the policy structure and coverage selected.

4. Policy Issuance

Personal accident policies are issued by insurers after proposal review.

Paisabada Financial Services facilitates policy purchase through authorised insurers.

5. Claims

Customers may submit claims directly to the issuing insurance company.

Paisabada may assist in claim documentation where applicable.

6. Important Disclosure

Insurance is the subject matter of solicitation. Please read policy documents carefully before purchase.

Paisabada Financial Services is an Authorised POS Insurance Facilitator. Insurance policies are issued by respective IRDAI-licensed insurance companies. We do not underwrite insurance policies or guarantee claim settlement. Final underwriting and claim decisions rest solely with the respective insurer.
